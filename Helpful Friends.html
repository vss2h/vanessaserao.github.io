Multi-Lingual Dictionary For children's Ebook Application

Prototype

Overview

The use of engaging technology applications, including eBook reading among children and their parents, is increasingly common.

For child readers, a major component of learning outcome—both from paper and digital formats—is vocabulary acquisition and eBooks that present words challenging the reader’s vocabulary have the potential to expand vocabulary over time.

The word definition utility will facilitate smoother reading experiences for parent-child interactions. The proof-of-concept system will initially focus on providing definitions for English, French, and Spanish but the system should be extendable to other languages in the future. The realization of such a tool could make eBook reading a much more effective and accessible experience for children.
